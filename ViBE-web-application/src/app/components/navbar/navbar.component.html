    About ViBE

    Growing Together

    This site was put together in late 2019 by a group of students from the East Coast area taking the "Advanced Web Technology course" originally designed to enteract with a database and allow people of like minded interests to connect with each other.


    We are ecstatic to continue our learning journies in tech.


    Building this site has inspired us to push our abilities as developers to the next level! We hope it might help you find and share opportunities for inspiration, learning, growth, and for your community as well.
